Mesopotamian Mythology

Mythological Figures

Enlil

God of the air, wind, and storms.

Ishtar

Goddess of love, beauty, war, and political power.

Marduk

Patron god of Babylon, associated with justice and order.

Traditions and Festivals

Akitu Festival

Celebration of the new year and the renewal of life.

Zagmuk Festival

Celebration of the victory of Marduk over Tiamat.

Esagil Festival

Celebration of the construction of the Esagil temple.
